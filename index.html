Kratos is a protagonist in one of my favorite game series' and one of playstations many exclusive games .

Kratos's backstory

kratos was once a respected Spartan warrior, he became the "Ghost of Sparta" after accidentally killing his family due to Ares' trickery. He later avenges the deaths of his family and becomes the God of War after killing Ares. Kratos is eventually revealed to be a demigod and the son of Zeus, who later betrays him.
